About MY IP PRO

What is this tool?

MY IP PRO is a comprehensive network utility designed to give you instant insight into your digital connection. Whether you are a developer debugging a network issue, a gamer checking latency, or a privacy-conscious user verifying a VPN connection, we provide the data you need in real-time.

Our Tools

IP Analysis

Detects your public IPv4/IPv6 address along with geolocation data (Country, City, ISP) and browser fingerprints.

Speedtest

Measures your network Latency (Ping), Download speed, and Upload speed directly from your browser without Flash or Java.

DNS Leak Check

Verifies which DNS server is handling your requests to ensure your VPN is effectively hiding your DNS queries.

WebRTC Detection

Checks if your browser is leaking your local LAN IP address through WebRTC technology.

Privacy & Data Policy

We believe in privacy first. Here is how we handle your data:

  • No Logging: We do not store your IP address or query history on our databases.
  • Client-Side Processing: Most checks (like History) are stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage).
  • Transient Data: Speedtest data is generated instantly and discarded immediately after the test.
